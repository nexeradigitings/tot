The Vibe

Charcoal walls.
Warm bulbs. Loud sauce.

TOT is Bank Square's answer to 2am cravings — a low-lit, high-energy den where the grill never really stops. String lights hang low over the tables, the wall art does most of the talking, and the smell of smoky bacon jam finds you before you find a seat. It's not a quiet dinner spot. It was never meant to be.
